Grade 9 Activities

VPOP 2023

VPOP 2023 was an experience. There had been a lot of misunderstandings in our class, because of this contest. We were all stressing and tired of practicing. We would practice during thee weekends and weekdays just to perfect our performance. We were all going to be graded by every single subject that we had, so we had to do our absolute best. Day of the contest came, we were all getting ready, dressing up, and fixing our costumes. Before we went down to the front of the Villar building, we all prayed for our performance to go smoothly. We worked severely to make sure that we could show our very best. Coming closer to our time to perform, we were all nervous. Holding hands to calm eachother down. Then it was our time. We sang and we danced like it was our last (last na po talaga HAHAHA). Of course, it was not perfect, but we did our best. After the performance, we sighed out of relief. Tapos sinabi pong next year i-aannounce yung winners, so we were all devastated. 2024 came in, and we were awarded Champion of the VPOP! I say dasurvv!

Variety Show

The variety show was really something. Throughout the whole process, we had a lot of ups and downs. It was just like VPOP, but even worse. This was even harder because there were different segments to practice and each of us had multiple segments. We would practice and practice until we got tired. The practices were hard, but they were quite fun. I was in the waltz segment, and it was my favorite part. Because of the VShow, I became fond of ballroom dances. I was also in the hiphop segment, but it was okay. I messed up a lot, but it's fine. We were all busy practicing, creating props, and writing songs. The day of the show we went to school before 7am. The performance would start at 8:30 and we were in the school's court. We were the only grade 9 section that would perfrom in the court, and all the sections would watch us so the pressure was extreme. The first part of the performance, we were all so tense. We were so nervous, because we had only practiced the whole show all-in-all once. There were a lot of mistakes, but natawid naman. As we were ending, the pressure and nervousness were starting to fade. We were all just enjoying and dancing to "Born This Way", kahit mali-mali yung steps. The Vshow was a great oppurtunity to showcase our talents. It was stressing, but we learned a lot throughout our journey in the variety show.
